About Us

This is basically a Library System and this Library System (LS) acts as a tool to transform traditional libraries into digital libraries. The primary objective of any library system is to collect, store, organize, retrieve and make available the information sources to the information users.Our Library System, we are facilitating to provide services to those who seek knowledge, wisdom and freedom. Like other Library System (LS), we are not categorized, separable or focused only a one group of people/users.
